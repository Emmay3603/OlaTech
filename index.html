Frequently asked questions

  • Can solar really power my A/C and Freezer?
    Yes, if sized correctly. We build strong systems specifically for A/Cs and freezers. We calculate your usage first to make sure the system handles the load without tripping.
  • Is solar actually cheaper than a generator?
    Yes. You save huge money. You stop buying fuel and paying for repairs. The system pays for itself in about few months, then you enjoy free electricity for years.
  • How long do the batteries last?
    It depends on the type. Tubular batteries last about 3–5 years, while Lithium batteries can last over 10 years. We help you choose the one that fits your budget.
  •  Do you offer warranties?
    Yes, absolutely. We give warranties on panels (up to 2 years), inverters, and batteries. If there is any issue with our installation, we fix it immediately.
  • I already have an inverter. Can you add solar to it?
    Usually, yes. We can add solar panels to your current inverter so your batteries charge faster than using NEPA alone.
